Why SoCalNMT?

Often times pain can be caused by myofacial triggerpoints. Triggerpoints are hyper-irritable bands of muscle tissue. Triggerpoints can form due to injury, lack of movement, repetitive movement, poor posture and other reasons. Over time Triggerpoints can cause pain also known as myofacial pain syndrome.
